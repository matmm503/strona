About Me

My name is Joanna and I come from Poland. I have been living in the Netherlands for 16 years. You know that friend who is always the photographer of the group? That was me. A few years ago I decided to change my hobby into a job as a professional photographer.

At first I was photographing newborns, children's birthdays but with time I started taking portraits of women and men. And here I discovered what I wanted to do in my life – capturing the emotions of the people I photograph in their happy and important moments. Or maybe showing them for who they really are, something not many people know about. Creating for others is my passion.

I invite you to contact me.
