About Me

My name is Joanna and I come from Poland. I have been living in the Netherlands for 16 years.

You know that friend who is always the photographer of the group? That was me. A few years ago I decided to change my hobby into a job as a professional photographer.

I started by photographing newborns and birthday celebrations, but over time I discovered that portrait photography is what I truly love. Capturing genuine emotions and real moments is what inspires me most.

I create images that reflect people as they are, capturing their happiest and most honest moments - this is where my passion lies.
